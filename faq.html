Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Roots to Blooms and how to get your plants.

Where are you located?

Roots to Blooms is located at 960 Barrington Street, Halifax NS.

What are your hours?

Our Hours of Operation not including holidays, are:

  • Monday-Wednesday: 7pm-9pm
  • Thursday: closed
  • Friday: 7pm-9pm
  • Saturday: 9am-5pm
  • Sunday: 9am-2pm
Can I visit your store?

Unfortunately, no. Roots to Blooms is not a "Brick and Mortar" store. You are not able to browse plants in person. However, we can take plenty of pictures of the plants you are interested in. We want you to be happy with your purchases!

How do I get the plants that I want to buy?

Curbside pickup or delivery. When you checkout (hover over Shop, then select Houseplants), you will be redirected to a Google Form. After submitting, we will contact you shortly.

Generally, pickup or delivery is during our normal Hours of Operation. We try to be accommodating, but Roots to Blooms is a part-time business, so plants are usually not sold between 8am-5pm, Monday-Friday.

How much is delivery?

Delivery fees depend on your location:

  • Halifax, Dartmouth, Spryfield: $5
  • Bedford, Sackville (Middle and Lower), Bayer's Lake, Timberlea, Eastern Passage: $8

We will confirm a specific delivery time after receiving your order. If you need to reschedule, please contact us as soon as possible.

When we arrive, we will wait 15 minutes for you to respond. If no response, your order will be returned and delivery fees may apply for each delivery attempt.
