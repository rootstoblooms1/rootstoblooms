What is our mission?

We strive to produce high-quality houseplants through propagation, growing plants from cuttings taken from healthy parent stock. Our goal is to provide strong, vibrant plants while helping others discover the joy of growing.

How do I purchase plants?

Visit the shop page and add plants to your cart. When you're ready, checkout and complete the order form. We'll contact you shortly after submission.

We currently accept cash and Interac e-Transfer.
